Researching AI that's fair,

I'm a PhD research scholar at the Yardi School of Artificial Intelligence, IIT Delhi, advised by Prof. Sandeep Kumar. My research lies at the intersection of Federated Learning, Fairness in ML, Graphs.

Before IIT Delhi, I completed my Master's in Communication from Bharti School of Telecommunication, IIT Delhi. I am broadly interested in building AI systems that are fair, trustworthy, and grounded in theoretical guarantees.
